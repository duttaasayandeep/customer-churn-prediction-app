Banking Customer Churn Prediction

What is Churn Prediction?

Churn prediction is a data-driven process used to identify customers who are likely to leave a company. In the banking sector, understanding and anticipating churn can help banks retain valuable customers, improve customer service, and increase profitability.

This prediction app leverages machine learning to analyze customer behavior and financial data, thereby empowering bankers to make informed decisions and develop effective customer retention strategies.
